Comfortable Rooms

We are an Australian company specializing in mid-term shared accommodation for young professionals, travelers, and students seeking a comfortable place to call home while living abroad. While the majority of our properties are premium and luxurious, we also offer affordable room options.

7-Day Guarantee

We are committed to customer satisfaction. We stand behind all our services with a robust 7-day guarantee. Our dedicated Customer Service support team will go the extra mile to address your concerns and ensure you have the experience you deserve. However, if you are not happy after 7 days, you can leave without penalty.

Fully Furnished Properties

We believe that accommodation should not be a worry for our clients, so we offer homes that are fully equipped with everything needed. Additionally, we can prepare an entire home just for you and your group. And if you ever feel like a change of scenery, you can move to another home in a different suburb free of charge.

Who we are?

Your next housing ally

  • March 2018

    The Beginning

    One sunny day in Melbourne, a group of friends gathered to discuss the challenges they faced as international students in Australia. Frustrated by the lack of comfortable and affordable housing options, they had an epiphany. They envisioned creating a welcoming community for students by offering beautifully furnished properties.

  • November 2022

    An Agency is Born

    Our company's inception was the result of a shared passion for creating exceptional living experiences for international students. Fueled by a collective vision of transforming standard accommodations into vibrant, community-driven spaces, a group of like-minded individuals came together to establish our enterprise.

  • January 2023

    First Room Rental

    We achieved a significant milestone by renting out our very first room to an international student. It was a moment filled with excitement and anticipation as we took the first step towards realizing our vision. This marked the beginning of our journey to provide comfortable and welcoming accommodations to students from around the world.
